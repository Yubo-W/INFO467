EXPLORE

Our Mission

“Offering a unique way to explore UW dorms using a VR platform.”

Features

Our platform is designed with the user in mind, tailoring the experience to fit their needs and providing a new way to explore unique UW dormitories around campus by implementing it on a virtual reality platform.

Using our platform, users will be able to see a real life scale of the miltuple room configurations within the UW dormitories. They will also be able to see all of the furniture that is provided when the user lives here, and have the ability to customize the room by moving the objects around. Not only will they be able to see the different types of rooms, they will also have their own personal tour guide to help them navigate the virtual tour.

Most importantly, students that use our platform will be able to see multiple room configurations in real life scale. Seeing a real life scale room in an immersive 3D environment is not possible with a static image.

Providing foregin and freshmen students with a chance to tour the dorms before deciding where to live

Acessible across multiple platforms, including VR platforms, traditional computers, and web browsers

Using the latest technology to create a virtual bridge between the user and the UW dormitories

Cost Analysis

Not only will our platform provide a way for students to tour the campus in an unique way, it is also not very costly.

For example, users only need a computer, smartphone, or a VR headset. Most people have one of these already. Also, a cheap way to get a VR headset is to use Google Cardboard (costs less than $5) and send these out to incoming students in their acceptance offer letter. Users will also need access to an internet connection, which most users around the world have as well.

Target Audience

Our platform is intended to help new incoming students to explore what it is like to live with the UW dormitories.

Not all students live close enough to Seattle to come to campus to see these dormitories in person. Using our platform, it will allow these students to have the opportunity to get the same experience as the students who come to UW for a tour.

Cheaper and less time consuming for students who live far away (especially international students)

For students who want to find the perfect dormitory room for them during their time at the UW

Any inetersted student will have access to our platform, no matter where they are on the planet.

VIRTUAL REALITY

According to the Virtual Reality Society, virtual reality is:

"[near] what we expect experience as human beings."1
"...a virtual environment presented to our senses in such a way that we experience it as if we were really there."1

Our platform aims to provide the student with the necessary components to make a great VR experience possible ("hardware, software, and sensory synchronicity"1). Using all of these components, the student will truly believe they are inside a room in the UW dormitories.

When students use our platform, they are immersing themselves in an environment that is just like the UW dormitories. From being able to see an entire dormitory room in an extremely accurate 3D environment, to being able to customize and interact with items in the room just like in real life.
